Robot

The image below image shows my robot model rendered in Substance Painter after importing the final version.

The model was created for an assignment where we had to design a physical “puppet” capable of posing in a T-position that will then be imported into Mixamo for animation. My robot was first constructed physically using cardboard and hot glue — materials chosen for their accessibility and the raw, handmade aesthetic they bring to the piece. There’s a playful contrast in using such fragile materials to build a form typically associated with strength and technology.

Once assembled, I scanned the robot using LiDAR, imported the scan into Blender, and reduced the polygon count to make it suitable for animation. The final model was exported as an OBJ file.
